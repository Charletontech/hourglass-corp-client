Who We Are

We are a member-owned and member-controlled cooperative society dedicated to supporting Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) in Nigeria. Our mission is to empower SMEs to succeed and thrive by providing access to resources, expertise, and opportunities. If you're an SME looking for a supportive community and access to resources and expertise, join us today! Together, let's build a stronger, more resilient business community.

Our Objectives

To facilitate networking and collaboration opportunities among members, fostering inter-personal relationships and all-round development.

To provide access to affordable financing and business support services for SMEs while advocating for policies and initiatives that support SME growth and development.

To offer training and capacity-building programs for SMEs. Building a generation of intellectually sound and skillful individuals.
